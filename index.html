Game Library

Only play games you own. ROMs are stored privately on this device (IndexedDB) — nothing is uploaded. Saves & save states are kept in browser storage; use the in-game save buttons.

⏩ Speed-up mode

In game, tap the FF button in the top bar to fast-forward at 3×, or use the in-game settings menu. Perfect for candy-grinding trips to the PC.

Rare Candy cheats

The app reads each ROM's header and auto-detects the game. Supported games (Ruby → White 2, incl. most ROM hacks built on them) get verified Rare Candy codes offered at launch — they land in the in-game cheat manager, ready to toggle.

🤖 Claude Auto-Play

Let Claude play the Nuzlocke for you. It watches the screen and presses the buttons itself, streaming its reasoning live. Works best on GBA games (FireRed, Emerald, Ruby) — buttons only, no DS touch. Add your Anthropic API key, start a game, then tap AI in the emulator top bar.

Tips

UPS/BPS/xdelta patches verify checksums — if you get a warning, your base ROM revision probably doesn't match (e.g. FireRed v1.1 vs v1.0). Most FireRed hacks need FireRed (U) v1.0; Emerald hacks need Emerald (U); DS hacks like Renegade Platinum ship as .xdelta and need the US ROM. Hardcore favorites: Emerald Kaizo, Renegade Platinum, Blaze Black/Volt White (caps for all of these are in the Rules tab).
